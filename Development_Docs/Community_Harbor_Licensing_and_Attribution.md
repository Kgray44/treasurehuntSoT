# Community Harbor licensing and attribution

Built-in policy keys are `ALL_RIGHTS_RESERVED`, `USE_IN_CHRONICLES_WITH_ATTRIBUTION`, `REMIX_WITH_ATTRIBUTION`, `NONCOMMERCIAL_REMIX_WITH_ATTRIBUTION`, `REDISTRIBUTE_UNMODIFIED_WITH_ATTRIBUTION`, `PUBLIC_DOMAIN_OR_CC0_STYLE`, and `CUSTOM`. Each policy records private/public use, modification, redistribution, commercial use, attribution/source-link/share-alike requirements, policy version, active state, and custom terms where applicable. These keys are platform policy labels, not unqualified assertions of Creative Commons legal compatibility.

Every release stores an immutable license snapshot and ordered attribution snapshot. Attribution holds credited profile when available, immutable display credit, contribution type, required credit text, source reference, asset exception seam, and sort order. Profile suspension/deactivation never rewrites historical credits. An asset can later carry a stricter policy or separate source attribution without changing release history.

Ownership declarations record declarant profile, version, timestamp, accepted statements, and optional evidence references. They state author permission and attribution acknowledgement but do not provide legal verification. Future remix compatibility must compare the release snapshots, not mutable Creator defaults.
